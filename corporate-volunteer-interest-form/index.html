Corporate Volunteer Interest Form

Thank you for your interest in coordinating a group volunteer opportunity for your organization. Metro United Way (MUW) is committed to connecting groups to meaningful volunteer experiences that advance the fight for the education, financial independence and health of every person in our community.

We look forward to working with you to create an impactful volunteer experience that meets real needs in our community. Please take a few minutes to complete the form below with as much detail as possible and we will follow up with you soon to discuss project plans.
