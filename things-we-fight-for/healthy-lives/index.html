HEALTH MATTERS

In just a 25 minute drive across Louisville from east to west, there's a 12 year life expectancy difference because of racial disparities. That's not okay with us and we're working to addresses root causes and ensure that the zip code you're born into doesn't dictate the kind of life you have and certainly not how long you live. We're committed to build a stronger, healthier and more equitable future for all.

Get down with the basics

By providing a network of services for people’s most basic needs of food, shelter, health and safety, we can ensure that children and families have not only what they need, but also what they need to know to live healthy lives.
