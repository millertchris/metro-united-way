2020 Was The Year...

They say hindsight is 20/20 and they were right. 2020 didn’t go as planned, but it did bring our community needs more into focus and it’s clear the time to do something bold is now.

Metro United Way is uniquely positioned to bring us all together, in addressing our community’s biggest issues and creating a stronger, more equitable future for all.
